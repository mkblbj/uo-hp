# Message from the Representative

> ***We want to bring a touch of the extraordinary to the items people use every day.***

In 2015, when we launched our online store, we began selling smartphone cases.  
Because these are items familiar to everyone, we wanted them to offer more than convenience alone. We wanted them to bring **the joy of choosing and the pleasure of self-expression**. That idea became **our starting point**.

From the beginning, we have developed **custom-made smartphone accessories** as a core focus, always valuing **the idea of adding small moments of enjoyment and color to everyday life**.  
Because these are things people use every day, we want them to fit naturally into daily life and personal style, and to become **products that people continue to choose over time**.

At the same time, what we value is not only the products themselves, but also **careful customer support** and **the relationships we build with customers**.  
The **knowledge and insight we have accumulated through making customers happy in actual sales settings** has become an important foundation of UO Co., Ltd.

Today, while continuing to make use of the experience we have built in the smartphone accessories field, we are also expanding into new areas such as **e-commerce sales of domestically produced foods in Japan**.  
Going forward, we will continue to broaden the possibilities of our business so that we can deliver **value that supports everyday life** in a wider range of forms.

## What We Value

::: tip
- Adding the joy of choosing and a small sense of uniqueness to familiar everyday products
- Building long-term relationships with customers instead of ending the experience at product delivery
- Connecting the knowledge gained from actual sales operations to future product development and business expansion
:::
