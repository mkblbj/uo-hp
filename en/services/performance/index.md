# Sales Performance

<PerformanceAwardBadges />

Since opening a store on Rakuten Ichiba in 2019, UO Co., Ltd.'s stores have been used by many customers.  
This page organizes the strengths of the operational foundation behind the smartphone accessories business through sales performance and evaluation indicators.

::: info What you'll find on this page
- Major sales results centered on Rakuten Ichiba
- Customer trust as reflected in evaluation indicators
- Operational strengths supporting these results
:::

## Major Results

| Item | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Store Launch | Opened on Rakuten Ichiba in 2019 |
| 2021 Sales (actual) | JPY 100 million |
| Average Review Rating | 4.59 (as of March 31, 2026) |
| Number of Reviews | 16,755 (as of March 31, 2026) |
| Rakuten Ichiba Ranking (past result) | Reached 31st among all stores |

> Note: The average review rating and number of reviews are based on public information for Matsutake Shoten on Rakuten Ichiba as of March 31, 2026.  
> Note: The store launch date, 2021 sales, and Rakuten Ichiba ranking reflect past achievements.

## What the Results Show

- After launch, the business achieved **rapid growth in sales scale** at a relatively early stage
- Both the **review rating** and the **number of reviews** indicate steadily accumulated customer satisfaction
- These results come not only from sales volume, but also from the combined strength of **store operations** and **customer care**

## Operational Strength Supporting the Results

UO Co., Ltd. has continually worked not only on selling products, but also on improving product pages, building up reviews, and enhancing the quality of customer support.  
These day-to-day operational improvements connect directly to both sales performance and customer evaluations.

In particular, within the smartphone accessories business, the foundation has been built on consistent store operations supported by **multi-device support**, **small-lot handling**, and an **integrated system linking processing through sales**.
