The Domestic Foods Business is a **new pillar that UO Co., Ltd. is actively nurturing**.  
Its aim is to rediscover the value of agricultural products and foods produced within Japan and deliver them carefully to consumers, with **"国内産屋"** as the central brand.  
Built on the **e-commerce operating capabilities** cultivated through the smartphone accessories business, the company is also developing a new sales foundation in the food category.

## Business Concept

- Expand distribution volume by **delivering foods produced in Japan to customers in Japan and overseas**
- Present attractive regional ingredients and processed foods in a way that is easy to understand
- Use existing **e-commerce operating know-how** to cultivate a new sales category

## Progress

- **April 2024**: Launched as a new project
- **July 2024**: Filed trademark registration for "国内産屋"
- **February 2025**: Acquired the trademark for "国内産屋"

## Current and Planned Product Categories

- Citrus fruits (Wakayama, Ehime, and more)
- Fruit
- Rice (locally produced rice from different regions)
- Processed foods (including additive-free products)
- Alcoholic beverages (sake, shochu, and domestic wine)
- Seafood (under consideration)

> *国内産屋 is the brand that symbolizes our Domestic Foods Business.  
> It is being developed as a sales base for rediscovering the value of agricultural products and processed foods from across Japan and delivering them to consumers.  
> Going forward, we plan to strengthen how products are presented so customers can better understand them, while also improving operations that lead to repeat purchases.*


## Future Direction

Using the **sales and operational capabilities** built through the smartphone accessories business, we will also establish an operating structure in the food category that supports **product understanding, value communication, and repeat purchases**.  
Going forward, we plan not only to expand our product categories, but also to improve brand presentation and the overall sales experience.
