Based on the **e-commerce operating know-how** developed through Rakuten Ichiba and Amazon, UO Co., Ltd. plans to expand its product categories and strengthen business coordination, including collaboration between Japan and China.  
As an extension of its existing businesses, the company will also work on **AI- and IT-driven support for e-commerce operations** and the **development of business support agents**, aiming to further strengthen its business foundation.

- Product areas expected to expand in the future
- The direction of AI and IT use supporting e-commerce operations

:::

## Product Area Expansion

In addition to existing smartphone accessories, the company is considering expansion into the following **lifestyle-supporting product categories**.

- Food and alcoholic beverages
- Electrical appliances
- Stationery
- Clothing

## AI and IT Support for E-commerce Operations

Based on the operating experience built through the existing e-commerce business, the company plans to expand into **business support using AI and IT**.  
Specifically, the idea is to apply these tools in practical ways starting from areas close to day-to-day e-commerce operations, such as organizing product information, improving pages, assisting customer responses, and streamlining operational work.

## Development of Business Support Agents

In the future, the company is also considering the **development of IT tools and agents that support day-to-day e-commerce operations**.  
By using the sales and operational know-how accumulated in-house, the company aims to build systems that support product understanding, inquiry handling, and workflow organization, and to develop them as a **practical foundation supporting the e-commerce business**.

## Use of Existing Know-how

- **Sales and operational know-how** from Rakuten Ichiba and Amazon
- Experience in improving product pages and managing reviews
- Operational design for launching new categories
- A **business improvement perspective** based on hands-on sales operations

## Strengthening Japan-China Coordination

UO Co., Ltd. is not only focused on domestic sales in Japan, but is also expanding its business with **order coordination and product listing cooperation with partners on the China side** in mind.  
Going forward, the company plans to continue strengthening its **business foundation, including Japan-China coordination**, alongside expansion into new product areas.
