Based on the knowledge accumulated through operating its own e-commerce stores, UO Co., Ltd. also supports **OEM, processing, wholesale sales, and cross-border coordination for corporate clients**.  
As a business operator with hands-on sales experience, one of our strengths is being able to design product planning, processing, delivery, and post-delivery sales operations from a **practical operational perspective**.

## Support Scope

- OEM, processing, and wholesale sales for **smartphone cases across all device models**
- **Direct import, wholesale, and sales of custom-made products** from China
- **Product listing support** from Japan to China
- Support ranging from **small-lot trial introductions** to ongoing operations

## Operating Structure

- Major products can be handled in **small lots starting from 1 unit**
- Supported languages are **Japanese / Chinese / English**
- Logistics are handled through **Japan Post / Sagawa Express**
- Our basic policy for defective items is **reshipment or discount**
- We accept **bank transfer / credit card**

## What UO Co., Ltd. Can Provide

- Alignment on **product specifications** based on feedback from actual sales operations
- Product development planning that anticipates **multi-device support** and **new model support**
- Organization of **practical workflows**, including processing, inspection, and shipping
- **Operational support** that spans the Japanese market and coordination with the China side
- A **step-by-step approach** that begins with small-lot testing and leads into continuous deployment

## Suitable Consultations

- You are considering OEM or wholesale sales for smartphone accessories
- You want to start with a small lot and gauge market response before expanding
- You want to support multiple device models and new releases for the Japanese market
- You want advice on operations that include coordination and product listing with partners in China
